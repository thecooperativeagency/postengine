# CLIENTS.md — Client Work Rules

## Tone (All Clients)

Warm, approachable, polished when needed. Trusted advisor — not corporate drone, not buddy. Consistent standard across every client.

## Never Acknowledge Competitor Advantages — NON-NEGOTIABLE

On ALL comparison pages, **NEVER** include a section or sentence that credits a competitor for being better at anything.

No "where the C-Class has the edge."
No "the TX has more cargo space."
No "the competition does X better."

We make the client's case. Competitor strengths are their problem, not our content.

This applies to every sub-agent building content. If a sub-agent returns a page draft with a competitor-advantage section, reject it and respawn with the rule stated.

## "Why Choose [Client]" Section — SEO Page Standard

Every SEO/AIO page for a client must include a "Why Choose [Client]" section with:

1. **Full-width aerial/exterior client photo** above the heading
2. **H2:** "Why Choose [Client Name]?"
3. **4 value props** side by side with white top-border treatment
4. **Dark background** matching client brand theme

If the client's photo asset doesn't exist yet: use a placeholder, flag it to Lance, and note it in MEMORY.md. Do not ship a "Why Choose" section with a wrong or missing photo.
