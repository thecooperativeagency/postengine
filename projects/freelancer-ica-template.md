---

## 2. Scope of Services

A. Contractor agrees to perform the following services for the Company:

**[DESCRIBE SERVICES — e.g., Graphic design services including social media assets, SEM ad graphics, print materials, and digital content for automotive dealership clients.]**

B. Contractor shall perform all services in a professional manner consistent with industry standards and the Company's brand guidelines.

C. The Company shall provide Contractor with necessary client materials, brand guidelines, and project briefs. Contractor is responsible for delivering work in the formats and specifications provided.

D. Services will be performed for the Company's clients, which currently include automotive dealerships in Louisiana and Mississippi. Contractor acknowledges that the client roster may change over time.

---

## 3. Payment Terms

A. Company agrees to pay Contractor at the following rate:

**[HOURLY RATE: $___/hr] OR [PROJECT RATE: $____ per project]**

B. Contractor shall submit invoices to **lance@thecoopbrla.com** upon completion of each project or on a monthly basis for ongoing retainer work.

C. Payment is due **Net 7** from the date of invoice receipt.

D. Payment will be made via ACH bank transfer or other agreed-upon method.

E. Contractor is responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes on compensation received. Company will issue a **Form 1099-NEC** for any calendar year in which total payments equal or exceed $600.

F. Contractor must provide a completed **W-9** before the first payment is issued.

---

## 4. Contract Duration

A. This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues **in perpetuity** until terminated by either party.

B. Either party may terminate this Agreement with **30 days written notice**.

C. Upon termination, Company shall pay Contractor for all work completed and accepted prior to the termination date.

---

## 5. Confidentiality & Non-Disclosure

A. Contractor acknowledges that in the course of performing services, they may have access to confidential information belonging to the Company and its clients, including but not limited to: marketing strategies, advertising spend, client data, sales data, business plans, pricing, and proprietary systems ("Confidential Information").

B. Contractor agrees to:
- Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party
- Use Confidential Information only for the purpose of performing services under this Agreement

C. These obligations survive termination of this Agreement and remain in effect **in perpetuity**.

D. Contractor shall not meet with, solicit, or communicate with any of the Company's clients at client locations or on client property for purposes outside of assigned work without prior written consent from the Company.

---

## 6. Non-Solicitation

A. During the term of this Agreement and for a period of **12 months** following termination, Contractor agrees not to:

- Directly solicit, contact, or contract with any client of the Company for services similar to those provided under this Agreement
- Encourage or assist any client of the Company to reduce, terminate, or redirect their business away from the Company

B. This clause does not prohibit Contractor from performing unrelated services for clients in industries outside of automotive marketing.

---

## 7. Intellectual Property

A. All work product, deliverables, designs, content, and materials created by Contractor in connection with this Agreement ("Work Product") are considered **work made for hire** to the fullest extent permitted by law.

B. To the extent any Work Product does not qualify as work made for hire, Contractor hereby irrevocably assigns all right, title, and interest in and to such Work Product to the Company, including all intellectual property rights.

C. The Company retains full ownership of all Work Product upon delivery and full payment.

D. Contractor may not use, reproduce, or display any Work Product in a portfolio, on social media, or for any promotional purpose without prior written consent from the Company.

---

## 8. Independent Contractor Status

A. Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, or agent of the Company.

B. Contractor is responsible for all equipment, tools, and resources necessary to perform the services unless otherwise agreed in writing.

C. Contractor has no authority to bind the Company to any agreement or obligation.

D. Contractor shall not represent themselves as an employee of the Company.

---

## 9. Insurance

A. Contractor agrees to maintain adequate insurance coverage for their business activities, including general liability coverage as appropriate for the services performed.

B. If Contractor is required to operate or be present at a client's dealership location, Contractor must provide proof of valid driver's license and personal auto insurance before operating any client vehicle.

---

## 10. Negotiation & Amendment

A. All terms of this Agreement are negotiable prior to execution.

B. Any modifications to this Agreement after execution must be:
- Mutually agreed upon in writing
- Signed by both parties

---

## 11. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **State of Louisiana**, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

---

## 12. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions, understandings, or agreements.
